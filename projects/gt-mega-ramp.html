GT Mega Ramp

Android
Offline 3D Car Racing Stunts Ramps
About the Game
  • Took over and rebuilt an existing ongoing Unity project from the ground up, stabilizing and completing it after inheriting a broken codebase.
  • Integrated NWH Vehicle Physics controller for realistic formula car handling and stunt mechanics.
  • Developed multiple game modes including stage-based stunt levels with finish line progression and a height map mode with challenging uneven terrain from bottom to top.
  • Implemented a garage system with up to 12 unlockable formula cars.
  • Built a flip and stunt system supporting front, back, and side (left/right) mid-air flips.
  • Integrated Google AdMob with a rewarded ad system allowing players to respawn at their last position instead of restarting the full stage.
  • Added graphics quality settings (Low, Medium, High) for performance optimization across devices.
